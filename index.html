Ed Sheeran

Early Life

Ed Sheeran was born in England in 1991. He grew up in a small town called Framlingham, Suffolk. Ed began singing in the church choir at age for. At age 11, he began playing the piano. His parents encouraged him to pursue his musical talents. By age 16, he left school and moved to London. While playing at small venues and recording his songs, he struggled to get by.

Becoming a Hit

Ed was finally discovered in 2011 when he released his debut album, “plus”. His most popular song was “The A Team”. In 2014, he released “multiply” which included the song “Thinking Out Loud”. His third album was released in 2017, and was called “divide”. This album was his most popular.

Now

Ed continues to release new music albums to this day, and remains a popular figure in the music industry. He contributes to charities and lives a life consistent with the lyrics of his own songs. Ed has mastered the art of storytelling through music, and he continues to tell stories for his listeners, even now.
